A New Era of Spaceflight

In May 2020, SpaceX successfully launched the Crew Dragon spacecraft carrying NASA astronauts to the International Space Station. This was the first crewed space mission launched from the U.S. since 2011, and the first privately built spacecraft to carry humans.

Fun Facts

  • Doug Hurley and Bob Behnken were the first astronauts to fly on Crew Dragon.
  • It was launched on a Falcon 9 rocket from Kennedy Space Center.
  • This mission marked the beginning of commercial human spaceflight.
