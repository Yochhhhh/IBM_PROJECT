A Revolution in Technology

In 2007, Steve Jobs unveiled the first iPhone, combining a phone, an iPod, and an internet communicator into a single device. It set new standards for smartphones with its sleek design and touch-based interface.

Fun Facts

  • The first iPhone had no App Store — apps came later in 2008.
  • It featured a 2MP camera and only 16GB storage at maximum.
  • Steve Jobs introduced it with the words: “An iPod, a phone, and an Internet communicator.”
